    Your focus determines your reality.

    In this case, would procrastination be my dark side?

    At Nokia, I had purpose.

    Of course, even now that my internship has ended, I find work to occupy myself and ways to continue creating and thinking, but at Nokia, I had purpose. Responsibilities, expectations, goals. Tasks that had some level of necessity and importance. And during this time, my motivations peaked.

    At first, a lot of my work consisted of graphic design: posters, banners, e-mail templates... the works.

    However, over time, I was given both more responsibility and more freedom. As a result, I was able to create such amazing assets.
