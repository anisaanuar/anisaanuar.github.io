Anisa's Extraordinary Virtual Puzzle Hunt (AEVPH)

For my 22nd birthday, I wanted to do something that channeled every aspect of my identity. It was my first birthday in quarantine, and I had to work with my virtual limits. Very few virtual escape rooms had existed that early on (July 2020), and I wanted something that as many of my friends could enjoy as possible. A couple of weeks later, AEVPH was born!

I had quite a bit of experience in creating a website from just HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and working quickly (as long as it's something I'm passionate about) is my strength. It only took me two weeks to get AEVPH up and running in time for people to play through it - including all the assets and graphics. Each puzzle had a special attachment to my identity, whether it be my favorite game (crossword puzzles) or set in my favorite place (Dunkin' Donuts).
