AEVPH (Virtual Puzzle Hunt) ★

Skills used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, asset creation, puzzle making and mapping, user testing

A 22-level virtual puzzle hunt that I created to celebrate with my friends! Complete with a variety of puzzles, from crossword to Sudoku to Codenames, creating this from scratch was a fun and satisfying adventure.

MBTA App Redesign ★

Skills used: Benchmarking, user flow diagramming, UI specification, VD specification, prototyping

Taking an app that fails to simplify the complicated system of the MBTA and an app that succeeds at making tooth-brushing into something fun and rewarding and combining the two to make something beautiful.

Radical Healing App ★

Stardew Valley Calendar ★

Stardew Valley is a beautiful game, and overwhelming in so many ways. It has endless features, and so so much to keep track of. This calendar is intended to help farmers in the game monitor the growth cycle of crops so they can optimize planting. It also shows how much crops are worth to help the farmer consider their profits as well.

Rock Climbing Infographic Posts ★

Rock climbing is one of my favorite pastimes, and I love introducing new people to the sport. This infographic shows the basics of climbing, from the holds to the harnesses to the shoes. I enjoyed this experiment of making information something accessible, unique, and visually pleasing, and testing my abilities as an artist!

Apparel Design

I've learned that team spirit often comes from having unity. Apparel for large groups can bring people together in a way that few other things can, whether it be taking a large group photo with everyone wearing the same shirt, or showing up to a random event and seeing someone else wearing the same hoodie. It's that sense of "we're in this together" that I loved designing for.
