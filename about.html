About Me

Personal Details

Hi there! I'm Anisa. I'm a Malaysian-American fourth-year Computer Science and Design student at Northeastern University.

Although I'm still a novice at most skills, I find that, like most people my age, I have high aspirations. As a growing developer, designer, writer, and baker, I look forward to making the world a better place, one fixed bug, one em space, one slant rhyme or one chocolate chip at a time.

During my first few of years at Northeastern, I developed a strong interest in both artificial intelligence and user experience design. With the help of past managers, co-workers, peers and mentors, I have gotten closer to achieving my goals.

This website is a collection of my works.
